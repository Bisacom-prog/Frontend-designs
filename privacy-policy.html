Privacy & Cookie Policy

1. Who I am

This website is the personal portfolio of Bismark Apenkwah, a product designer (UI/UX & Front End). The site showcases my work and allows visitors to contact me about potential projects or collaborations.

2. What information I collect

I may collect and process the following types of information:

  • Contact details you provide via the contact form, such as your name, email address and message.
  • Technical information such as your IP address, browser type, device information and approximate location, if analytics tools are enabled.
  • Usage data about how you interact with the site (pages visited, time on page, clicks) when analytics cookies are allowed.

3. How I use your information

  • To respond to your enquiries and messages.
  • To maintain and improve the website, design, and content.
  • To understand interest in my work and services.
  • To comply with any legal or regulatory obligations where applicable.

4. How your information is stored

Messages sent via the contact form are delivered to my email inbox (via Formspree or a similar service) and are stored there as long as reasonably necessary for our communication and records. I do not sell your data or share it for marketing purposes with third parties.

5. Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to help the site function and to collect information about how visitors use it. Some cookies are strictly necessary, while others are optional (for analytics and personalisation).

I group cookies into the following categories:

  • Strictly necessary cookies – required for basic site operation and security. These are always active.
  • Analytics cookies – help me understand how visitors use the site so I can improve it.
  • Marketing / personalisation cookies – support any embedded third-party media or future promotional content.

Where required by law, analytics and marketing cookies will only be set on your device if you provide consent through the cookie banner or the “Manage Cookies” settings.

6. Managing cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by clicking “Manage Cookies” in the footer.Most web browsers also allow you to block cookies or delete them via your settings. Please note that rejecting certain cookies may affect the functionality or performance of the website.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights over your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your information. If you have any questions or requests regarding your data, you can contact me using the form on this site.

8. Contact

For privacy or data-related enquiries, please reach out via the contact form on this website and mention “Privacy Request” in your message.

This policy is intended as a general explanation of how this personal portfolio handles information and cookies. If you need formally vetted legal wording, please seek independent legal advice.
